🍞 Peanut Butter Toast

The quickest and most filling breakfast — creamy peanut butter on crispy toast!

Easy ⏱ Prep: 2 mins 🔥 Cook: 3 mins 🍽️ Serves: 1

🛒 Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread (any kind)
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter (creamy or crunchy)
  • 1/2 banana, sliced (optional but recommended!)
  • A drizzle of honey (optional)
  • A pinch of cinnamon (optional)
  • A few chia seeds or sesame seeds (optional)

📋 Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Toast the bread slices in a toaster or on a pan until golden and crispy.
  2. Spread 1 tablespoon of peanut butter generously on each slice.
  3. If using banana, arrange the slices on top of the peanut butter.
  4. Drizzle a little honey on top if you like it sweeter.
  5. Sprinkle a tiny pinch of cinnamon for extra flavour.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy your quick breakfast!

💡 Tips

  • Whole wheat bread makes this healthier and more filling.
  • Try adding sliced strawberries or apple instead of banana.
  • Peanut butter + honey combo is absolutely amazing — don't skip it!
  • This is also a great post-workout snack for protein!
