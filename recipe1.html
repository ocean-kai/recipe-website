🍜 Maggi Noodles

The classic college student go-to meal — hot, tasty, and done in minutes!

Easy ⏱ Prep: 2 mins 🔥 Cook: 8 mins 🍽️ Serves: 1

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1 pack of Maggi noodles (with tastemaker)
  • 1.5 cups of water
  • 1 small onion, chopped (optional)
  • 1 small tomato, chopped (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp butter or oil
  • Salt to taste
  • A pinch of red chilli flakes (optional)

📋 Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Boil 1.5 cups of water in a pan on medium heat.
  2. If using vegetables, add onion and tomato now and cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Add the Maggi noodle cake into the boiling water.
  4. Add the tastemaker (masala) packet from the Maggi pack.
  5. Add a little butter or oil for extra flavour.
  6. Stir everything well and cook for 2–3 minutes till the water reduces.
  7. Turn off the flame. The Maggi should be slightly saucy — not too dry!
  8. Serve hot in a bowl and enjoy 😊

💡 Tips

  • Don't overcook — Maggi goes soggy if left too long.
  • Add cheese on top for a cheesy version!
  • You can also add boiled egg or paneer cubes.
