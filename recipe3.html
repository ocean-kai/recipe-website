🌽 Corn Chaat

A spicy, tangy street-style snack that's super healthy and tasty!

Easy ⏱ Prep: 5 mins 🔥 Cook: 5 mins 🍽️ Serves: 2

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1 cup boiled sweet corn kernels (canned or fresh)
  • 1/2 tsp chaat masala
  • 1/4 tsp red chilli powder
  • 1/4 tsp cumin powder (jeera powder)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • A few coriander leaves (optional)
  • 1 tsp butter (optional)

📋 Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. If using fresh corn, boil it in water for 5–7 minutes. If using canned corn, just drain the water.
  2. Heat a pan and add butter. Once melted, add the corn kernels.
  3. Toss on medium heat for 2 minutes until slightly roasted.
  4. Turn off the heat. Add chaat masala, red chilli powder, cumin powder, and salt.
  5. Mix everything well. Squeeze in the lemon juice.
  6. Transfer to a bowl and top with chopped onion and coriander leaves.
  7. Serve warm and enjoy your street-style corn chaat!

💡 Tips

  • Roasting the corn a little gives it a nice smoky flavour.
  • You can also add diced tomato for extra freshness.
  • Sprinkle some sev (crispy noodles) on top for crunch!
