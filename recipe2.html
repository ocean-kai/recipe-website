🎂 Oreo Mug Cake

Only 2 ingredients! This microwave cake is perfect when you need dessert in 2 minutes.

Easy ⏱ Prep: 3 mins 🔥 Cook: 2 mins 🍽️ Serves: 1

🛒 Ingredients

  • 6 Oreo biscuits
  • 3–4 tablespoons of milk
  • Optional: a pinch of baking powder for fluffier texture
  • Optional: chocolate chips or whipped cream for topping

📋 Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Take 6 Oreo biscuits and crush them into fine crumbs in a mug or bowl.
  2. Add 3–4 tablespoons of milk to the crumbs.
  3. Mix well until it forms a smooth batter — it should be thick but pourable.
  4. Pour into a microwave-safe mug (don't fill more than 3/4 full).
  5. Microwave on HIGH for 60–90 seconds.
  6. Let it cool for 30 seconds before eating (it will be very hot!).
  7. Add toppings if you like — chocolate chips, cream, or even more crushed Oreos!

💡 Tips

  • Every microwave is different — start with 60 seconds and check.
  • If the cake looks wet in the centre, give it 15 more seconds.
  • Use flavoured Oreos (like strawberry) for a fun twist!
  • Adding a pinch of baking powder makes it more fluffy and cake-like.
