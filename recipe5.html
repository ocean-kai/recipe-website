🍏 Green Apple Mojito

A super refreshing mocktail with mint, apple, and a citrusy kick!

Easy ⏱ Prep: 8 mins 🥤 No Cook 🍽️ Serves: 2

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1 green apple, peeled and diced
  • 8–10 fresh mint leaves
  • 2 tablespoons sugar (or sugar syrup)
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 cup chilled soda water or sparkling water
  • Ice cubes (as needed)
  • A pinch of black salt (optional, but recommended!)
  • Mint sprig and apple slice for garnish

📋 Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Dice the green apple and put it in a blender with a little water. Blend into a smooth puree.
  2. Strain the apple puree through a sieve to get clear apple juice. Keep aside.
  3. In a glass, add sugar and lemon juice. Stir until the sugar dissolves.
  4. Add the fresh mint leaves and gently muddle them (press with a spoon) to release the flavour.
  5. Add ice cubes to fill the glass halfway.
  6. Pour the green apple juice over the ice.
  7. Top up with chilled soda water and gently stir.
  8. Garnish with a mint sprig and an apple slice on the rim.
  9. Serve immediately and enjoy your refreshing mocktail! 🍏

💡 Tips

  • Use very cold soda for the best fizzy experience.
  • A pinch of black salt gives it a lovely chaat-style tangy twist!
  • You can also add a dash of green food colour for a fun look.
  • For a sweeter version, add more sugar syrup.
